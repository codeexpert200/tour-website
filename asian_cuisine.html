Ravi Restaurant

Ravi Restaurant is a Pakistani restaurant, which serves some of the less budget feasting at its finest with some of the famous dishes like 'Chicken Jalfreizi', which is packed full of spices and swimming with vegetables and chunks of meat. Apart from that, the atmosphere is great filled with mixed crowd.

Location: Al Dhiyafa Road

Timings: Saturday-Friday: 5AM-3AM

Contact: 04-3315353

Calicut Paragon Restaurant

Calicut Paragon Restaurant is an Indian restaurant. It is seafood that reigns supreme here while there are plenty of meat and vegetarian dishes. It is known very well for 'Crab Tushar' seafood dish and 'Peralan Chicken' dish. It is an absolutely unmissable brilliant budget restaurant.

Location: Amman Street

Timings: Saturday-Friday: 7AM-12AM

Contact: 04-2988858
