Maraheb Restaurant

Maraheb is a Yemeni restaurant which has several branches in Dubai. They mainly serve chicken, meat, and seafood dishes, they are very well known for a famous dish in Arabic called 'Mandi', which consists mainly of rice, meat (or chicken) and a mixture of spices. Aside from that they also serve salads, juices, beverages and sweets.

Location: International City

Timings: Saturday-Friday: 12PM-12AM

Contact: 04-2514442

Al Nafoorah Restaurant

Al Nafoorah is an award winning Lebanese restaurant. From Lentil Soup to Lamb Chops to Hookah, the restaurant is known for serving some of the finest and most authentic Lebanese food in Dubai.

Location: Sheikh Zayed Road

Timings: Saturday-Friday: 12PM-12AM

Contact: 04-3198760
